About Me

I am a mechanical engineering student in my third year at Texas A&M with a senior level status. I intend to complete a minor in mathematics soon and eventually earn a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering. Currently, I am on the university's SAE Aero Design Team, involved in Formula SAE, and I work on projects for my professor, Dr. Rainer Fink. Through these extracurriculars, I have become an advanced user of Solidworks and various other programs.

Career Interests: engineering, automotive industry, electric vehicles, aerospace industry, and sustainable energy.

Goal: To be part of a mission, if not leading it, that is pushing the world forward with innovation.

My Engineering Philosophy: As an engineer, I shall always strive to adhere to priciples of sustainable development while holding paramount the safety, health, and welfare of the public.
